Naše usluge / Our Services
01
Instalacija tajmlaps kamere
Timelapse Camera Installation

Postavljamo industrijsku 24MP kameru trajno na vaše gradilište — otpornu na sve vremenske uslove, napajanu solarom, sa radom od −50°C do +60°C. Konekcija 3G/4G/5G, do 2TB memorije. Radi svuda.

Industrial-grade 24MP timelapse camera, permanently installed, weatherproof, solar-powered. 3G/4G/5G connectivity, up to 2TB onboard storage.

02
Gotov filmski proizvod
Finished Cinematic Film

Sirovi tajmlaps je samo početak. Naša produkcijska ekipa isporučuje kompletan video: tajmlaps sekvence, snimanje sa terena, aero snimci dronom, infografike, muzika i narativni okvir — funkcioniše i kao marketing i kao dokumentacija.

Raw timelapse is just the beginning. Complete film: timelapse sequences, operator footage, drone aerials, infographics, music and narrative — ready to publish.

Aerial & Drone Coverage

Dynamic aerial footage, panoramic overviews, photogrammetry, inspection. Flight range 5km, up to 90 km/h.

All cameras in one dashboard, mobile app, video archive, CMS integration. Embed a live site feed on your website with a single click.
